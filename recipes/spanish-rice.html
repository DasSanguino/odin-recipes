Spanish Rice

About this Spanish Rice

This is a Tex-Mex inspired rice dish that pairs perfectly with birria, beans, salsa, or other fixings to your taste.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups White Rice
  • 2 cups Water
  • 1 can Diced Tomatoes and Chilis
  • 1 TBSP Caldo con Sabor de Tomate
  • 1 TBSP Canola Oil
  • 2 cloves Garlic

Steps

  1. Heat oil in nonstick pan and fry rice until translucent
  2. Add garlic and sautee until browned
  3. Add all remaining ingredients at once
  4. Cover pot with tight fitting lid and simmer on low for 25 minutes
  5. Fluff rice and serve
