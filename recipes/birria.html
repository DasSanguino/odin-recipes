Birria

About this Birria

This is a non-traditional birria, simplified for a weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs Beef Stew Meat
  • 1 Onion, sliced
  • 4 Cloves Garlic, crushed
  • 1 TBSP Brown Sugar
  • 1 TBSP Caldo con Sabor de Pollo
  • Dash of red wine vinegar or lime juice
  • 3 Dried Guajillo peppers
  • 1 or 2 Jalapenos, coined
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a large pot or dutch oven with the lid closed
  2. Bake at 275 f for 3-4 hours, checking and stirring occasionally
  3. Remove the now hydrated guajillo peppers and blend them with some of the juice from the pot. Add back to the pot once smooth
  4. Shred the beef to your desired consistency
  5. Allow the beef to sit for at least 20 mins to absorb the broth
