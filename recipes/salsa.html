Salsa

About This Salsa

This is a Tex-Mex salsa, which is blended into a puree and used more as a condiment.

Many elements of this salsa may be adjusted to your taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 Red Bell Pepper
  • 2 Jalapenos
  • 1 10 oz Can Fire Roasted Tomatoes
  • 4 Cloves Garlic
  • 1/2 Bunch of Cilantro
  • Salt to Taste

Steps

  1. Peel the garlic, slice the peppers in half and de-seed the bell pepper and one of the jalapenos
  2. Roast the bell pepper, both jalapenos, and two of the cloves of garlic on a foil lined sheet at 350 f for 15 minutes
  3. Once roasted, broil until charred to your taste
  4. Once cool enough to work with, blend roasted vegetables with all remaining ingredients and add salt to taste
  5. Chill for 6+ hours for optimal flavor development
